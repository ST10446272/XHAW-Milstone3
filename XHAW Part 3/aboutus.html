About Empowering The Nation

Empowering The Nation is a local small to mid-size enterprise, a initiative by Precious Radebe to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners.

Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg to hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners on both the :
Six - Month Long Learnerships and
Six - Week Short Skills Training.

Empowering The Nation's training provides upskills to domestic workers and gardeners by making them more marketable when seeking employment and also enables them to be paid at a higher rate because of these additional skills they posses.

The training school offers opportunities of becoming your own entrepreneur and setting up your own small business utilising these newly obtained skills. Moving towards the goal of filling the occupational vacuum there is regarding many employers requiring their employees to be upskilled to offer more skilled services of the job.

Our Programs

The Six - Months programmes take place over Tweleve Weeks and the short courses take Six weeks

Six - Months

  • First Aid
  • Sewing
  • Landscaping
  • Life Skills

Six - week

  • Child Minding
  • Cooking
  • Garden Maintenance

Motivation

Precious was inspired to start this business by her parents and other elderly relatives who were never given the chance to upskill themselves or follow a formal educational qualification, so in starting this business she offers the opportunity to similarly affected members from her community.
