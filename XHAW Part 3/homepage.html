Background of our business

Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg. Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes to empower themselves and can provide more marketable skills.

As an institution we deal with physical skills
For instance :

  • Child Minding
  • Cooking
  • Garden Maintenance

The list that is mention above is actually moduls for our six week course. More information will be found in the courses page.

Discounts

Customers (employers or employees) can select from a range of courses available. They can select more than one course. The more courses the customers select the more discount they receive.

  • One course – no discount
  • Two courses – 5% discount
  • Three courses – 10% discount
  • More than three courses – 15% discount
